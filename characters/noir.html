Biography

Before anyone knew the name Noir, governments knew her only as a file number.

Raised and trained within a covert Dutch intelligence programme, she became one of Europe's most effective field operatives. Silent. Efficient. Emotionless.

Until she was ordered to eliminate innocent people.

Walking away made her one of the agency's most wanted former assets, forcing her into a life spent hiding in plain sight.

Deronimo recognised her talent immediately and attempted to recruit her. His mistake was assuming she still took orders.

Now she enters the King of Fists Tournament for the same reason she walked away from her old life: to ensure nobody like Deronimo ever controls people again.

Special Moves

Signature Special: Shadow Step

Noir disappears into darkness before reappearing behind her opponent with a lightning-fast strike.

Secondary Special: Black Tulip

A graceful spinning kick followed by razor-sharp precision attacks that reward perfect timing.

Super Move: Silent Contract

The arena falls silent as Noir dismantles her opponent with flawless assassin techniques before vanishing into the darkness once more.

Transformation: Phantom Operative

Her movement becomes almost invisible, dramatically increasing evasion, speed and counter attacks.

Finisher: No Witnesses

The lights go out. When they return, Noir is already walking away while her opponent collapses behind her.
