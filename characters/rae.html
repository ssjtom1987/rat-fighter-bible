Biography

Growing up serving steins in Düsseldorf’s busiest beer halls made Rae strong long before she ever entered a gym.

Years of lifting kegs, breaking up fights and carrying trays through crowds gave her the kind of strength that looks impossible until she throws someone across a room.

Rae’s unusual ability to increase her size and power under stress made her a legend across Europe’s underground fighting scene. The harder she is pushed, the stronger she becomes.

Deronimo once attempted to use her family’s bar as a front for weapons trafficking. Rae refused. His men destroyed the business in retaliation.

That was their second mistake. The first was assuming Rae would stay down.

Special Moves

Signature Special: Keg Toss

Rae lifts a full beer keg and hurls it across the arena, smashing through projectiles and opponents alike.

Secondary Special: Iron Shoulder

A heavy shoulder charge that absorbs light attacks and knocks opponents backwards.

Super Move: Oktober Slam

Rae grabs the opponent and delivers a devastating series of wrestling slams before launching them into a festival strength machine.

Transformation: Giant Mode

Rae grows larger and stronger, gaining massive throw damage, armour and increased reach.

Finisher: Beer Barrel Blitz

Hundreds of barrels roll through the arena, flattening the opponent before Rae raises a stein to the crowd.

Arcade Ending

With Deronimo defeated, Rae returns to Germany and rebuilds her family’s bar from the ground up.

This time, it becomes more than a beer hall. It becomes a training ground, a refuge and a place where nobody pays protection money to anyone.

She later wins an international strength competition, breaks three records and opens a gym beside the bar.

The first rule is written above the entrance:

"No cowards. No smugglers. No skipping leg day."
