Biography

Little is known about the man beneath the black sheep mask. Those who claim to have seen his face either vanish or refuse to speak of it ever again.

Born somewhere within the hills of County Cork, Deronimo built an international criminal empire through fear, manipulation and absolute secrecy. Arms dealing, smuggling, illegal genetics research, underground fighting and political corruption all lead back to one name.

Unlike most crime bosses, Deronimo is not driven by money. He is obsessed with strength. For decades, he has used the King of Fists Tournament to study the world’s greatest fighters, recruit the useful, and destroy the dangerous.

His greatest creation was Rat Fighter. His greatest mistake was inviting so many people whose lives he had already ruined.

Special Moves

Signature Special: Black Sheep Charge

Deronimo lowers his head and rushes forward with brutal force, smashing through guards, walls and opponents alike.

Secondary Special: Crime Syndicate

Masked henchmen briefly rush the arena, striking from the shadows before disappearing back into the crowd.

Super Move: King's Judgement

Deronimo overwhelms the opponent with precise, cruel strikes before smashing them through his throne.

Transformation: Lord of the Black Sheep

His mask cracks, red light burns through the fractures, and dark energy surrounds him as his attacks become heavier and harder to interrupt.

Finisher: The King's Execution

The castle gates slam shut. Deronimo silently walks away while the arena collapses behind him.
