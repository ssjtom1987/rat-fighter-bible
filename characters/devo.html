Biography

Raised among abandoned factories, rusting cars and unforgiving streets, Devo learned early that nobody was coming to save him.

His fists became his education. His toughness became his currency. His loyalty became the one thing nobody could buy.

Despite his intimidating size and reputation, Devo is not a cruel man. Most of the time, he is cheerful, easy-going and quicker to laugh than fight.

Unfortunately, that changes when someone crosses the line.

When pushed too far, something inside Devo snaps. The calm disappears, the smile fades, and the berserker takes over. In that state, he becomes a wrecking ball with fists.

Deronimo's organisation once tried to recruit him as hired muscle. When Devo refused, they went after people close to him.

They survived.

Deronimo's men did not.

Special Moves

Signature Special: Berserker Punch

Devo plants his feet and throws a crushing overhand punch that can blast through an opponent's guard.

Secondary Special: Engine Rev

Devo pounds his fists together, briefly powering up his next strike with explosive force.

Super Move: Midwest Mayhem

A brutal rushdown combo that ends with Devo clotheslining the opponent through a pile of junkyard scrap.

Transformation: Berserker Rage

Devo loses control, gaining huge attack power and forward pressure while sacrificing some defence.

Finisher: Wrecking Ball

Devo hurls the opponent into a monster truck, then smashes them through it with one final rage-fuelled punch.

Arcade Ending

After Deronimo's defeat, Devo returns to the Midwest carrying more scars than trophies.

The criminal network that once tried to use him is gone, but Devo knows there are plenty of angry kids growing up the same way he did.

So he opens a free gym inside an abandoned warehouse.

No contracts. No egos. No one gets turned away.

He teaches boxing, discipline and the one lesson he had to learn the hard way:

"Being strong means knowing when not to swing."
