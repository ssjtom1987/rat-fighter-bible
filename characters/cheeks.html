Biography

Formerly one of Britain's most respected military operatives, Cheeks walked away from active service after uncovering corruption that reached far higher than anyone was willing to admit.

Refusing to become another obedient soldier, he opened a private investigation agency where he earned a reputation for taking cases no one else would touch. His calm manner, razor-sharp instincts and unwavering moral compass quickly made him one of the country's most respected detectives.

Long before either man entered the King of Fists Tournament, Cheeks played lead guitar in a rock band alongside Tom. The two remained lifelong friends, even after Tom abandoned music to hunt the mysterious crime lord known as Deronimo.

Over the years, Cheeks uncovered fragments of Deronimo's criminal empire hidden across Europe. Every lead ended the same way—witnesses disappeared, evidence vanished and entire investigations were buried.

When the invitation to the King of Fists Tournament arrived, Cheeks knew it wasn't a coincidence. This wasn't simply another case. It was his chance to finally expose the man hiding behind the Black Sheep mask.

Special Moves

Signature Special: Detective's Justice

Secondary Special: Flashbang Entry

Cheeks throws a tactical flash grenade before rushing forward with a devastating shoulder tackle.

Super Move: Case Closed

A cinematic military takedown combining joint locks, precision strikes and a finishing flying knee that sends the opponent crashing through a police car.

Transformation: Special Forces

Cheeks removes his detective coat, revealing his old military combat gear. His movement speed, counters and combo potential all increase dramatically.

Finisher: No Escape

After a relentless pursuit through the stage, Cheeks cuffs the opponent before delivering one final knockout punch.
