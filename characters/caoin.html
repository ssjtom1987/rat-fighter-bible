Biography

Gifted with a voice that could silence an entire pub, Caoin is known across Dublin as both a beloved bard and a dangerous man to challenge after closing time.

By day, he sings traditional ballads, plays golf badly with complete confidence, and enjoys a pint of Guinness like it is a sacred ritual. By night, he is one of Ireland's sharpest underground fighters.

Caoin's fighting style blends footwork, rhythm and sudden bursts of power. He moves like a musician keeping time, striking between beats and punishing anyone who fails to read the tempo.

Years ago, Deronimo attempted to recruit him into his criminal network. Caoin laughed in his face. Shortly after, several friends connected to the local music scene vanished without explanation.

The King of Fists invitation was not a surprise. It was a warning. Caoin accepted anyway.

Special Moves

Signature Special: Celtic Crescendo

Caoin steps forward with a rhythmic kickboxing combo that builds in speed before ending with a sharp rising knee.

Secondary Special: Guinness Splash

Caoin tosses a pint across the arena, briefly staggering the opponent before following up with a heavy roundhouse.

Super Move: Ballad of Battle

Caoin sings a powerful note that stuns the opponent, then launches into a brutal flurry of strikes timed perfectly to the music.

Transformation: Emerald Spirit

Green Celtic energy surrounds him, increasing his speed, rhythm-based combos and counterattack potential.

Finisher: The Last Pub Song

The whole pub joins in song as Caoin delivers one final uppercut, launching the opponent through the roof into the Dublin night.

Arcade Ending

With Deronimo defeated, Caoin returns to Dublin carrying more than victory.

He carries names. The names of friends, musicians and ordinary people who disappeared beneath the Black Sheep's shadow.

Weeks later, he performs before the largest crowd of his life. No sponsors. No spectacle. Just a stage, a microphone and a song written for those who never came home.

As the final chorus rises, pubs across Ireland join in.

For one night, the whole country sings.

And somewhere in the hills of Cork, the ruins of Black Sheep Castle fall silent.
