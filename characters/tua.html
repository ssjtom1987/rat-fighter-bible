Biography

From the jungles of Guyana to the mean streets of New York, Tua has travelled further than most fighters could imagine.

Short in height but enormous in reputation, he is a master of spear combat, survival tactics and silent movement through impossible terrain.

Years ago, Deronimo attempted to purchase an ancient tribal relic believed to hold enormous historical significance. Tua refused.

The village protecting it was attacked soon after.

The relic survived. So did Tua.

He enters the King of Fists Tournament carrying the memory of home and a promise that some things are not for sale.

Special Moves

Signature Special: Spear Throw

Tua hurls his spear with pinpoint accuracy, striking from long range before calling it back to his hand.

Secondary Special: Jungle Leap

A sudden acrobatic leap that lets Tua reposition, evade attacks and strike from above.

Super Move: Spirit Hunter

An ancient warrior spirit joins Tua’s assault, surrounding the opponent with spear strikes from every direction.

Transformation: Jaguar Warrior

Tua channels the spirit of the jungle, gaining enhanced movement speed, sharper spear techniques and improved aerial attacks.

Finisher: Temple Guardian

Ancient stone spears erupt from the ground as the jungle itself rises to protect what Deronimo tried to steal.

Arcade Ending

With Deronimo defeated, Tua returns the stolen relics to his homeland.

For the first time in years, the village gathers without fear of raiders, collectors or criminals arriving with money and guns.

Tua places the relic back where it belongs, deep within the temple ruins beside Kaieteur Falls.

He is offered wealth, fame and protection.

He refuses all of it.

Some treasures are not meant to be owned.
