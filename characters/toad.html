Biography

Toad was not meant to survive.

Captured as part of one of Deronimo’s illegal genetics programmes, she became the unwilling subject of Project Amphibia: an experiment designed to fuse human strength with amphibian mutation.

The scientists expected compliance. They got rage.

The experiment gave her the ability to transform into a monstrous toad-like form at will, increasing her strength, reach and durability beyond anything the laboratory could contain.

Deronimo believed she died when the facility burned.

He was wrong.

Special Moves

Signature Special: Tongue Lash

Toad strikes from range with a brutal mutant tongue whip, pulling opponents closer or interrupting attacks.

Secondary Special: Toxic Splash

She spits a toxic burst across the arena, creating a dangerous zone that punishes careless movement.

Super Move: Amphibian Fury

Toad transforms mid-combo into her mutant form, smashing the opponent through shattered laboratory glass and containment tanks.

Transformation: Mega Toad

Her body mutates into a hulking amphibious monster with increased power, armour and extended reach.

Finisher: Fly Feast

A giant tongue drags the opponent into the darkness as the broken lab lights flicker out one by one.

Arcade Ending

After Deronimo falls, Toad does not celebrate.

She searches his archives and finds the locations of every remaining genetics facility connected to Project Amphibia.

One by one, the labs go dark.

Records are destroyed. Prisoners are freed. Scientists vanish into courtrooms or hospital beds.

When the last facility burns, Toad disappears into the wilderness.

For the first time, nobody is watching her. Nobody is testing her. Nobody is telling her what she is.

She is free.
