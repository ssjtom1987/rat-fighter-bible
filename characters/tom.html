Biography

Once destined for sold-out arenas, Tom abandoned fame after uncovering evidence linking several disappearances within the music industry to Deronimo's criminal empire.

His relentless pursuit cost him everything: his band, his career and almost his sanity. Now he travels the globe mastering martial arts, following every lead that might bring him closer to the masked crime lord known as The Black Sheep.

For Tom, the King of Fists Tournament is not about glory. It is the final stop on a long road of obsession, revenge and unfinished business.

Special Moves

Signature Special: Guitar Smash

Tom summons an ethereal guitar and smashes it across the opponent with a burst of sonic energy.

Secondary Special: Encore Rush

A rapid martial arts combo finished with a spinning heel kick, designed to keep pressure on the opponent.

Super Move: Final Encore

A cinematic combo ending with a giant sonic shockwave from an exploding amplifier stack.

Transformation: Rock God

Tom's eyes glow blue, electric energy surrounds his fists, and his attacks gain extra speed and impact.

Finisher: Last Performance

Tom traps the opponent on a concert stage before every amplifier detonates, launching them into the crowd.
