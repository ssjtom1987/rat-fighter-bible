Biography

If there's music playing, chances are Mal is nearby.

Known throughout Ireland for his booming voice, quick wit and legendary tolerance for Guinness, Mal has spent decades entertaining packed pubs before accidentally becoming the toughest man in most of them.

Although often mistaken for a troublemaker, Mal would rather settle arguments with music than fists. Unfortunately, not everyone shares that opinion.

His friendship with Caoin has produced countless unforgettable performances... and almost as many friendly fights. Neither man remembers exactly who has won more.

When Deronimo's organisation began extorting local businesses throughout rural Ireland, Mal decided enough was enough. He entered the King of Fists Tournament to ensure nobody else would lose their livelihood to fear.

Special Moves

Signature Special: Pint Launcher

Mal hurls a full pint before charging behind it with a crushing shoulder barge.

Secondary Special: Lucky Clover Kick

A spinning kick surrounded by shimmering green energy that launches airborne opponents.

Super Move: Last Orders

The pub erupts into chaos as Mal delivers a devastating combination using stools, tables and fists before ringing the bell for closing time.

Transformation: Fighting Irish

Celtic energy engulfs Mal, dramatically increasing his resilience and combo power.

Finisher: Closing Time

The bartender calls last orders as Mal knocks the opponent clean through the pub doors and into the street.
