Biography

Deep beneath Brooklyn lies an abandoned genetics laboratory where experiments once blurred the line between human and animal.

When Deronimo discovered the ruins during a criminal operation in New York, only one experiment remained alive: half man, half rat, and entirely lethal.

Rather than destroy the creature, Deronimo raised him as his personal bodyguard, champion and adopted son. Rat Fighter was trained to know only loyalty, combat and survival.

For years, every fighter who reached the final stage of the King of Fists Tournament was sent into the sewers to face him. None returned victorious.

Rat Fighter has never known freedom. Only Deronimo. Only the tournament. Only the order to fight.

Special Moves

Signature Special: Sewer Rush

Rat Fighter drops low and charges forward with terrifying speed, clawing through anything in his path.

Secondary Special: Rabid Bite

A savage close-range attack that briefly stuns the opponent and leaves them vulnerable to follow-up strikes.

Super Move: Vermin Stampede

Rat Fighter lets out a screeching roar as hundreds of rats flood the arena and swarm the opponent.

Transformation: Alpha Rat

His body mutates further, claws lengthen, fur darkens, and his instincts take over. Speed and aggression increase dramatically.

Finisher: Sewer King

The ground collapses beneath the opponent, and Rat Fighter drags them screaming into the darkness below.

Arcade Ending

Deronimo falls.

For the first time in his life, Rat Fighter receives no command.

Months later, stories begin spreading through New York. Criminals vanish near the sewer entrances. Children speak of a giant rat-man who protects the innocent from the shadows.

Rat Fighter is no longer Deronimo's champion.

He is his own king.
