Biography

Kel works construction by day and trains by night, although nobody has ever accused him of doing either quickly.

Years of carrying timber, lifting steel and building stairs at a pace best described as geological have made him absurdly strong and remarkably hard to move.

He lives with Bob and helps care for him, although Bob insists this is unnecessary because he is “perfectly capable of causing problems by himself.” Their friendship is built on loyalty, insults and the occasional emergency trip to replace broken furniture.

When Bob decided to enter the King of Fists Tournament to confront Deronimo, Kel did not hesitate.

Where Bob rolls into battle, Kel walks beside him. Slowly. Very slowly.

Special Moves

Signature Special: Concrete Slam

Kel grabs the opponent and drives them into the floor with the full weight of a falling support beam.

Secondary Special: Stairway Driver

Kel launches the opponent upward with a rising shoulder charge, then slams them down like badly installed flooring.

Super Move: OSHA Violation

Steel beams, concrete blocks and loose tools rain across the arena as Kel delivers a devastating construction-site combo.

Transformation: Builder Mode

Kel puts on a hard hat and becomes harder to stagger, gaining armour and heavier throws.

Finisher: Demolition Day

The entire building site collapses around the opponent while Kel calmly checks whether the stairs are still level.

Arcade Ending

After Deronimo’s defeat, Kel returns home with Bob and quietly gets back to work.

But the communities damaged by Deronimo’s network still need rebuilding.

Kel starts a small construction company dedicated to repairing homes, gyms, venues and neighbourhoods ruined by crime.

He refuses to call himself a hero.

He just says the place needed fixing.
