Biography

Known online simply as Lemony Boi, he's the sort of person who can dismantle a security system before most people have finished making a cup of tea.

A lifelong programmer and technology enthusiast, he spends his days solving impossible technical problems and his evenings training MMA simply because he enjoys pushing himself.

Unlike many fighters, Lemony doesn't rely on raw aggression. Every movement is calculated. Every strike is chosen with purpose. Fighting, to him, is simply another system waiting to be understood.

While investigating unusual encrypted traffic on the dark web, he accidentally uncovered communications linking Deronimo's organisation to global cybercrime, surveillance and financial manipulation.

Instead of deleting the files, he copied everything. A week later, the King of Fists invitation arrived.

Special Moves

Signature Special: Lemon Launcher

A surprisingly powerful spinning kick followed by an exploding energy lemon that ricochets around the arena.

Secondary Special: Buffer Overflow

Lemony briefly freezes the opponent with a digital glitch before countering with a rapid MMA combination.

Super Move: System Crash

The arena glitches into cyberspace as Lemony overwhelms the opponent with lightning-fast combinations before finishing with a devastating spinning heel kick.

Transformation: Overclocked

His visor lights up as every movement becomes faster, smoother and almost machine-like.

Finisher: Blue Screen

The screen itself crashes before rebooting to reveal the opponent unconscious beneath a giant pixelated lemon.
