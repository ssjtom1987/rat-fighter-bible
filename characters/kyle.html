Biography

Kyle is a family man first and a fighter second. Unfortunately for his opponents, that still makes him very dangerous.

Raised in New Orleans and shaped by rap music, local gyms and street-corner confidence, Kyle developed a heavy boxing style built around pressure, rhythm and stubborn refusal to back down.

When Deronimo’s organisation attempted to extort businesses throughout New Orleans, Kyle stood in their way. That decision painted a target on his family.

Kyle entered the King of Fists Tournament not because he wanted fame, money or glory.

He entered because nobody threatens his people and walks away smiling.

Special Moves

Signature Special: Bayou Uppercut

Kyle ducks low and explodes upward with a heavy boxing uppercut strong enough to launch opponents off their feet.

Secondary Special: Mic Drop

Kyle feints with swagger before delivering a quick body shot that interrupts the opponent’s rhythm.

Super Move: Bourbon Beatdown

A boxing combo mixed with breakdance-inspired footwork, ending with a crushing hook beneath neon New Orleans lights.

Transformation: Family Man

Kyle refuses to go down, gaining stronger defence, heavier counters and increased damage when protecting a lead.

Finisher: Last Verse

Kyle drops the opponent through a New Orleans street stage as the crowd chants the final bar of his victory theme.
