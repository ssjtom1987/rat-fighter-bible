Biography

Don't let the height fool you.

Nina is the definition of controlled chaos. A devoted mother, lifelong gamer and fiercely loyal wife, she's the kind of person who can spend the afternoon laughing with friends and the evening flattening anyone foolish enough to threaten her family.

Growing up in New Orleans taught her to stand her ground. Life wasn't always easy, but she discovered early that confidence often wins before the first punch is thrown.

Unlike Kyle, Nina doesn't hesitate. If trouble appears, she's already halfway through solving it with her fists.

When Deronimo's organisation targeted those closest to her, she entered the King of Fists Tournament for one simple reason: to make sure nobody ever threatens her family again.

Special Moves

Signature Special: Mama Bear Rush

A relentless barrage of punches that grows stronger the longer the combo continues.

Secondary Special: Nightmare Spin

A spinning aerial kick inspired by her love of gothic fantasy that catches opponents trying to escape.

Super Move: Southern Stampede

Nina unleashes a devastating rush ending with a clothesline powerful enough to send opponents crashing through balconies.

Transformation: Tiny Juggernaut

Her eyes blaze with determination as her attacks gain armour and become almost impossible to interrupt.

Finisher: Family First

Nina launches the opponent skyward before smashing them back to earth with one earth-shaking final strike.
