Biography

Nobody knows whether Cartoon Mind is a costume, a personality, a curse or something far stranger.

What began as Tom's ridiculous skeleton alter ego during drunken nights out slowly evolved into something else entirely. The more Tom tried to bury his anger, frustration and self-doubt, the stronger Cartoon Mind became.

Unlike Tom, Cartoon Mind doesn't care about justice or revenge. He fights because it's fun. Every punch is a joke. Every victory is entertainment. Every defeat only makes him laugh harder.

His unpredictable behaviour makes him almost impossible to read. One moment he'll be dancing, the next he'll be flying across the arena with a spinning kick that nobody saw coming.

Some fighters believe Cartoon Mind is simply Tom wearing a costume after one drink too many. Others swear they've seen Tom and Cartoon Mind standing in the same room. Nobody can agree.

Special Moves

Signature Special: Bone Breaker

Cartoon Mind spins forward in a whirlwind of elbows and kicks before finishing with an exaggerated cartoon uppercut.

Secondary Special: Reality Glitch

He momentarily flickers out of existence before reappearing behind the opponent with a devastating strike.

Super Move: Saturday Morning Beatdown

The arena transforms into a cartoon world where Cartoon Mind bends reality, drops anvils, stretches his limbs and launches the opponent through impossible physics.

Transformation: Chaos Incarnate

His glowing eyes ignite bright blue as reality begins to distort around him. His attacks become faster, stranger and increasingly unpredictable.

Finisher: The Fourth Wall

Cartoon Mind notices the player, grins directly at the camera, then punches the opponent completely out of the game screen before casually walking away.
