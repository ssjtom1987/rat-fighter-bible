Biography

Growing up in Mumbai taught Joey B that survival often depends on thinking faster than everyone around you.

The city became his teacher. Crowded streets sharpened his reflexes. Food stalls built his legendary iron stomach. Back alleys, railway platforms and rooftop scraps became his training grounds.

Joey fights with confidence, humour and relentless momentum. He can slip through chaos like he was born inside it, because he was.

After dismantling one of Deronimo’s smuggling routes through India’s ports, Joey unknowingly earned himself an invitation to the King of Fists Tournament.

He accepted with a smile. If the road to justice leads through Cork, he is more than happy to make the journey.

Special Moves

Signature Special: Mumbai Rush

Joey dashes forward with a fast street-fighting combo, weaving through the opponent’s guard before landing a heavy body shot.

Secondary Special: Curry Bomb

Joey hurls a blazing street-food special that briefly stuns the opponent with heat, spice and regret.

Super Move: Street King

A relentless barrage of punches, elbows, knees and improvised attacks that ends with Joey launching the opponent through a market stall.

Transformation: Tiger Spirit

Golden tiger markings flare across his body, increasing his speed, pressure and counterattack damage.

Finisher: King of Mumbai

The entire market joins the chaos, sending the opponent bouncing through stalls before Joey delivers the final knockout.

Arcade Ending

Joey returns to Mumbai with Deronimo’s empire in ruins and a reputation larger than the city itself.

Instead of chasing fame, he opens a martial arts academy for children who grew up with nowhere else to go.

There are no polished floors. No fancy equipment. No luxury.

Just discipline, courage and a sign above the door:

"Fight for honour. Never for fear."
