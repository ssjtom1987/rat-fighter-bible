Biography

Frey has spent her entire life proving people wrong.

Albino, partially blind and constantly underestimated, she turned every doubt into discipline. What she lacks in perfect vision, she replaces with rhythm, instinct and ruthless timing.

Balancing music, performance and martial arts, Frey developed a flowing combat style that moves with the beat of a song. She reads vibration, breath and hesitation better than most fighters read footwork.

She crossed Deronimo’s path after exposing one of his trafficking operations hidden beneath a touring music festival. The invitation to King of Fists was not a challenge. It was a threat.

Frey accepted anyway.

Special Moves

Signature Special: Echo Strike

Frey listens for the opponent’s movement and counters with a sharp, perfectly timed palm strike.

Secondary Special: Northern Lights

A graceful spinning kick surrounded by shimmering aurora energy that can catch opponents trying to jump away.

Super Move: Valkyrie’s Song

Frey sings a haunting note as the arena darkens, then launches into a rapid aerial assault beneath glowing northern lights.

Transformation: White Wolf

Her eyes glow pale white, her speed surges, and her evasive movement becomes almost impossible to predict.

Finisher: Aurora’s Judgement

The Northern Lights descend into the arena, swallowing the opponent in a blinding wave of colour and sound.

Arcade Ending

After Deronimo’s fall, Frey returns to the stage beneath the Northern Lights.

Her victory becomes more than a headline. It becomes proof.

Across the world, people who were dismissed, underestimated or told they were too limited begin training, singing, fighting and standing taller.

Frey says very little about it.

She simply picks up her ukulele, smiles at the crowd, and begins the next song.
