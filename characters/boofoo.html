Biography

Boofoo would much rather be at home with a hot chocolate, surrounded by parrots, than standing in the middle of the world's most dangerous fighting tournament.

Soft-spoken, kind-hearted and usually underestimated by everyone around her, Boofoo has spent years caring for rescued birds and protecting endangered species from smugglers.

That quiet life ended when Deronimo's exotic animal smuggling operation targeted several birds under her protection. What his men expected to be an easy job became an embarrassing disaster involving broken noses, stolen keys and one very angry woman half their size.

Since then, Boofoo has trained herself into a lightning-fast acrobatic fighter, using speed, misdirection and sheer nerve to overcome opponents who think power is everything.

She enters the King of Fists Tournament not for glory, but to shut down Deronimo's animal trafficking network for good.

Special Moves

Signature Special: Feather Flurry

Boofoo darts forward in a blur of rapid strikes, moving so quickly she appears to attack from several angles at once.

Secondary Special: Parrot Peck

A loyal parrot swoops across the screen to distract and peck the opponent, creating a perfect opening for Boofoo to rush in.

Super Move: Birdstorm

The sky fills with parrots as Boofoo launches the opponent upward, letting the flock attack from every direction before she finishes with a diving kick.

Transformation: Phoenix Heart

Fiery wings flare behind Boofoo as her movement speed increases and her aerial attacks become far more dangerous.

Finisher: Sky Kingdom

A massive flock carries the opponent into the clouds before dropping them back into the arena with a humiliating crash.
