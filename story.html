Tournament Lore

General Story Overview

Every five years, an invitation appears in the hands of the world’s most dangerous fighters. No address. No organiser. No explanation. Just a black envelope bearing the mark of a sheep’s skull.

Inside are five words:

“Prove you are the strongest.”

Known only as the King of Fists Tournament, the event has become legend within the underground fighting world. Some fighters enter for glory. Some for revenge. Some because refusing the invitation is often more dangerous than accepting it.

The Man Behind the Mask

Behind the tournament stands Deronimo, an elusive Irish crime lord whose influence reaches across Europe, Asia and the Americas. To the criminal underworld, he is known as The Black Sheep.

Deronimo is not interested in fame, honour or fair competition. He uses the tournament to study, recruit and destroy the world’s strongest fighters. Those who impress him may be offered power. Those who refuse him tend to disappear.

His empire is built on smuggling, assassins, illegal experiments, black market technology, stolen relics and underground combat. Every fighter invited to the tournament has been touched by his organisation in some way.

The Champion: Rat Fighter

Standing between every challenger and Deronimo is his greatest creation: Rat Fighter.

Years ago, while conducting business beneath New York City, Deronimo discovered an abandoned genetics laboratory hidden inside the Brooklyn sewer system. Inside was a surviving experiment: a half-human, half-rat hybrid with terrifying speed, animal instincts and a natural talent for combat.

Rather than destroy the creature, Deronimo raised him as his personal champion and enforcer. Rat Fighter has defeated every challenger sent before him.

To reach Deronimo, a fighter must first survive the sewers and defeat the undefeated King’s Champion.

Why The Fighters Enter

This year, Deronimo has made one mistake. He has invited too many people with a reason to destroy him.

Revenge

Some fighters lost friends, family, careers or homes because of Deronimo’s empire.

Justice

Detectives, soldiers and former operatives enter to expose the truth and bring the network down.

Freedom

Deronimo’s experiments and former recruits fight to escape the shadow of the Black Sheep.

Glory

Some fighters simply want to prove they are the strongest warrior on Earth.

Tournament Structure

The tournament should feel like a journey through Deronimo’s empire, starting in familiar territory before descending into the criminal underworld.

Round 1

Home Ground
The fighter begins on a stage tied to their homeland or personal history.

Round 2

Rival Fight
A personal opponent, friend, enemy or mirror-match style challenge.

Round 3

International Invitation
The tournament expands across global stages and stranger opponents.

Round 4

The Black Market
Deronimo’s criminal network becomes visible.

Semi-Final

Brooklyn Sewers
The fighter must defeat Rat Fighter, Deronimo’s undefeated champion.

Final

Black Sheep Castle
The final battle against Deronimo on the cliffs of Cork.

The Tone

The world of Rat Fighter: King of Fists should take itself seriously, even when the characters are ridiculous. The humour comes from the contrast between dramatic arcade-fighter presentation and the strange, chaotic fighters caught inside it.

The goal is not parody. The goal is to feel like a lost fighting game from the late 90s: intense, over-the-top, stylish, weird, funny and weirdly sincere.

Core Story Beats

  • Deronimo secretly controls the King of Fists Tournament.
  • Every invited fighter has a connection to his criminal empire.
  • Rat Fighter is Deronimo’s undefeated champion and final obstacle before the true final boss.
  • Each fighter has their own rival, home stage and reason for entering.
  • Defeating Rat Fighter unlocks the final battle at Black Sheep Castle.
  • Each character receives a unique arcade ending after defeating Deronimo.
