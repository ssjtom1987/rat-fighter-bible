Tournament Lore

The King of Fists Tournament

Every five years, the world’s most dangerous fighters receive a black envelope marked with a sheep skull. No organiser. No address. No explanation.

Inside are five words:

“Prove you are the strongest.”

To the underground fighting world, the King of Fists Tournament is legend. Fighters enter for glory, revenge, justice, freedom, or because refusing the invitation is often more dangerous than accepting it.

The Black Sheep

Deronimo

Behind the tournament stands Deronimo, an elusive Irish crime lord whose influence stretches across Europe, Asia and the Americas.

Deronimo does not run the tournament for honour or sport. He uses it to study, recruit and destroy the world’s strongest fighters. Those who impress him are offered power. Those who refuse him disappear.

His empire is built on smuggling, assassins, illegal experiments, black market technology, stolen relics and underground combat. Every invited fighter has been touched by his organisation in some way.

The Champion

Rat Fighter

Standing between every challenger and Deronimo is his undefeated champion: Rat Fighter.

Years ago, beneath New York City, Deronimo discovered an abandoned genetics laboratory hidden inside the Brooklyn sewer system. Inside was the surviving result of a failed experiment: a half-human, half-rat hybrid with terrifying speed, animal instincts and a natural talent for combat.

Rather than destroy the creature, Deronimo raised him as his personal champion and enforcer. To reach the final battle, every fighter must first survive the sewers and defeat the King’s Champion.

Motivations

Why The Fighters Enter

This year, Deronimo has made one mistake. He has invited too many people with a reason to destroy him.

Revenge

Some fighters lost friends, family, careers or homes because of Deronimo’s empire.

Justice

Detectives, soldiers and former operatives enter to expose the truth and bring the network down.

Freedom

Deronimo’s experiments and former recruits fight to escape the shadow of the Black Sheep.

Glory

Some fighters want to prove they are the strongest warrior on Earth. Bad timing, really.

Arcade Ladder

Tournament Structure

The tournament should feel like a journey through Deronimo’s empire, starting in familiar territory before descending into the criminal underworld.

Round 1

Home Ground
The fighter begins on a stage tied to their homeland or personal history.

Round 2

Rival Fight
A personal opponent, old friend, enemy or mirror-style challenge.

Round 3

World Tour
The tournament expands across global arenas and stranger opponents.

Round 4

The Black Market
Deronimo’s criminal network becomes impossible to ignore.

Semi-Final

Brooklyn Sewers
The fighter must defeat Rat Fighter, Deronimo’s undefeated champion.

Final

Black Sheep Castle
The final battle against Deronimo on the storm-battered cliffs of Cork.

Creative Direction

The Tone

The world of Rat Fighter: King of Fists should take itself seriously, even when the characters are ridiculous. The humour comes from the contrast between dramatic arcade-fighter presentation and the strange, chaotic fighters caught inside it.

The goal is not parody. The goal is to feel like a lost fighting game from the late 90s: intense, over-the-top, stylish, weird, funny and strangely sincere.

Story Checklist

Core Story Beats

  • Deronimo secretly controls the King of Fists Tournament.
  • Every invited fighter has a connection to his criminal empire.
  • Rat Fighter is Deronimo’s undefeated champion and final obstacle before the true final boss.
  • Each fighter has their own rival, home stage and reason for entering.
  • Defeating Rat Fighter unlocks the final battle at Black Sheep Castle.
  • Each character receives a unique arcade ending after defeating Deronimo.
