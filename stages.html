World Tour

Stage Concepts

Each fighter has a home stage tied to their country, personality, backstory or connection to Deronimo. The stages should feel like dramatic 90s arcade arenas: bold, colourful, slightly ridiculous, and packed with background storytelling.

Main Boss Stages

Black Sheep Castle

Fighter: Deronimo

Location: County Cork, Ireland

An ancient castle on storm-battered cliffs. Lightning cuts through the sky while masked guards watch from the shadows. Beneath the castle sits Deronimo’s criminal vault.

Brooklyn Sewers

Fighter: Rat Fighter

Location: Brooklyn, New York

Flooded tunnels, broken pipes, graffiti, steam vents and glowing green runoff. Mutated rats watch from the darkness as Deronimo’s champion enters the arena.

Fighter Home Stages

The Last Encore

Fighter: Tom

Location: Abandoned Rock Arena

A once-famous concert venue left frozen in time. Broken guitars, old tour posters, flickering stage lights and unstable pyrotechnics surround the fight.

Midnight Investigations

Fighter: Cheeks

Location: Rainy City Detective Office

Rain lashes against the windows while neon lights flicker outside. Evidence boards, case files and red string point toward one name: Deronimo.

Forgotten Front

Fighter: Bob

Location: Battlefield Memorial

Old tanks, sandbags, falling rain and distant searchlights. A memorial stage where Bob’s past and present collide.

Steel & Concrete

Fighter: Kel

Location: Construction Site

A half-built skyscraper with cranes, sparks, concrete mixers and loose scaffolding. The perfect place for a slow builder with heavy hands.

Temple Bar Beatdown

Fighter: Caoin

Location: Dublin, Ireland

A packed Dublin street outside a traditional pub. Musicians play, crowds cheer, pints spill, and the whole city seems ready to join the chorus.

The Wild Shamrock

Fighter: Mal

Location: Irish Countryside Pub

Stone walls, low ceilings, old instruments, roaring fires and a crowd that treats every fight like closing time entertainment.

Neon Nightmare Carnival

Fighter: Cartoon Mind

Location: Unknown

A psychedelic Halloween fairground filled with warped rides, laughing skeletons, flickering signs and impossible cartoon physics.

Rust Belt Rumble

Fighter: Devo

Location: Midwest Junkyard

Rusting cars, monster trucks, oil drums and industrial machinery. Every background object looks like it could explode if someone punches hard enough.

Northern Lights Festival

Fighter: Frey

Location: Nordic Outdoor Concert

A night-time festival under the aurora. Snow falls softly while concert lights pulse through the mist and music echoes across the frozen ground.

Project Amphibia Lab

Fighter: Toad

Location: Secret Deronimo Laboratory

Broken tanks, glowing chemicals, smashed containment pods and giant mutant frogs suspended in the background. This is where Toad was changed forever.

Kaieteur Jungle

Fighter: Tua

Location: Guyana

Dense rainforest, ancient stone ruins, waterfalls and jungle wildlife. Spears, relics and hidden temple markings reveal a deeper history.

Neon Server Core

Fighter: Lemony Boi

Location: High-Tech Data Facility

A glowing server room filled with holograms, cables, robotic arms and collapsing security systems. The whole arena looks one bad line of code from disaster.

Amsterdam After Dark

Fighter: Noir

Location: Amsterdam Canals

Rain, neon reflections, bridges, bicycles and canal boats moving silently in the background. Beautiful, cold and dangerous.

Oktoberfest Arena

Fighter: Rae

Location: Germany

Beer tents, huge barrels, festival lights, cheering crowds and a strength-testing machine that nobody should stand too close to.

Bourbon Street Showdown

Fighter: Kyle

Location: New Orleans

Jazz clubs, street performers, graffiti, brass bands and late-night crowds. The fight feels like it could turn into a block party at any second.

Mardi Gras Mayhem

Fighter: Nina

Location: New Orleans

Parade floats, masks, beads, drums and festival colours everywhere. Bright, chaotic and dangerous once Nina loses her temper.

Paradise Aviary

Fighter: Boofoo

Location: Tropical Bird Sanctuary

Wooden bridges, waterfalls, tropical plants and hundreds of parrots watching from above. Looks peaceful. Absolutely is not.

Mumbai Central

Fighter: Joey B

Location: Mumbai, India

Busy railway platforms, food stalls, neon signs, crowds, trains and constant movement. The city itself feels like part of the fight.

Stage Design Notes

  • Every stage should clearly reflect a fighter’s personality or backstory.
  • Boss stages should feel more threatening and cinematic than regular stages.
  • Backgrounds can include animated spectators, weather, lighting changes and small visual gags.
  • Stages should feel like arcade arenas, not realistic locations.
  • Each stage could eventually have its own music theme.
  • Some stages could include subtle Deronimo clues, such as black sheep symbols, masked guards or hidden equipment.
