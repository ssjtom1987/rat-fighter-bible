Global Setting

The World of Rat Fighter

The King of Fists Tournament is not tied to one country, one city or one fighting scene. Deronimo's empire stretches across the world, pulling fighters from pubs, sewers, labs, jungles, docks, festivals, backstreets and railway stations into the same violent orbit.

This page tracks the wider world: where each fighter comes from, how their stories connect, and how Deronimo's criminal network links them all together.

Locations

Key Regions

County Cork

Home of Black Sheep Castle and the centre of Deronimo's power. The final destination for any fighter who survives the tournament ladder.

Brooklyn Sewers

The hidden underworld where Rat Fighter was discovered. A place of abandoned labs, toxic water, graffiti, steam vents and things that should have stayed buried.

New Orleans

Kyle and Nina's home turf. Music, family, street culture and Mardi Gras chaos collide with Deronimo's criminal expansion.

Mumbai

Joey B's city. Fast, crowded, colourful and impossible to control. Deronimo's smuggling routes made the mistake of passing through it.

Power Structures

Organisations

The Black Sheep Syndicate

Deronimo's international criminal empire. Its operations include smuggling, black market weapons, illegal science, assassins, stolen relics and underground fighting.

King of Fists Tournament

An invitation-only underground fighting tournament used by Deronimo to identify, recruit or destroy the world's strongest fighters.

Project Amphibia

A genetics programme connected to Deronimo's experiments. Toad escaped from it. Rat Fighter may be connected to its earlier research.

The Underground Circuit

The hidden global fighting scene where rumours of the tournament spread. Fighters enter through reputation, invitation or bad luck.

History

Tournament Timeline

Early Years

Deronimo builds the Black Sheep Syndicate from the hills of Cork into an international criminal network.

Brooklyn Discovery

Deronimo discovers Rat Fighter beneath New York and raises him as his champion.

Project Amphibia

Illegal mutation research creates Toad. The experiment fails when she escapes and destroys the facility.

The Invitations

Black envelopes are delivered to fighters across the world. Every invitation leads back to Deronimo.

The Present Day

The current King of Fists Tournament begins. This time, Deronimo has invited too many people with unfinished business.
