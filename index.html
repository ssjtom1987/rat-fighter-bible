The Game

Every five years, the world's most dangerous fighters receive an invitation to the King of Fists Tournament. Behind it all stands Deronimo, the masked Irish crime lord known as The Black Sheep. To reach him, each fighter must survive his champion: Rat Fighter.

Genre

Retro 2D arcade fighter inspired by Street Fighter, King of Fighters, Mortal Kombat and 90s fighting game chaos.

Tone

The world takes itself seriously. The characters do not always deserve the same courtesy.

Main Goal

Defeat Rat Fighter, reach Deronimo, and bring down the King of Fists Tournament.
