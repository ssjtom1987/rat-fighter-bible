The Premise

The King of Fists Tournament

Every five years, the world’s most dangerous fighters receive a black envelope marked with a sheep skull. No organiser. No address. Just an invitation to prove they are the strongest.

Behind the tournament stands Deronimo, the masked Irish crime lord known as The Black Sheep. To reach him, every fighter must first survive his undefeated champion: Rat Fighter.

Genre

Retro 2D arcade fighter inspired by Street Fighter, King of Fighters, Mortal Kombat and 90s arcade chaos.

Tone

The world takes itself seriously. The characters do not always deserve the same courtesy.

Main Goal

Defeat the roster, survive Rat Fighter, reach Deronimo, and bring down the tournament from within.
