Story Overview

Every five years, the world's most dangerous fighters receive an invitation to the King of Fists Tournament. Behind it all stands Deronimo, the masked Irish crime lord known as The Black Sheep.

To reach him, each fighter must first survive his champion: Rat Fighter.

Fighters

Tom

The Fallen Rockstar. A former musician turned martial artist hunting Deronimo.

Rat Fighter

Deronimo's half-human, half-rat champion from the Brooklyn sewers.

Deronimo

The Black Sheep. Crime boss, tournament master, and final opponent.
